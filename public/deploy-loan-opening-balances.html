  2. Click the orange "Loan Setup" button
  3. Fill in loan details:

  5. System automatically creates journal entries for proper accounting

Automatic Journal Entries Created:

  • Debit: Cash Account (1110) - Cash received when loan was taken
  • Credit: Loan Account (2210/2510/2530) - Liability recorded

Payment Integration:

  • When you make liability payments, loan balances are automatically updated
  • Current balance reduces by the principal amount of each payment
  • Complete audit trail maintained for all transactions
